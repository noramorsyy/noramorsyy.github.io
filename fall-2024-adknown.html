Work Term Report Fall 2024

Data Engineer Intern at Adknown

Hi I am back at Adknown for my second co-op term. My name is Nora and I am in my third year at the University of Guelph majoring in Software Engineering and minoring in Business and Project Management. I will share new projects, skills and challenges from this term and how I grew as a Data Engineer Intern.

About the Employer

Adknown is a leading digital media company based in Guelph Ontario focused on online advertising and data driven marketing solutions. The company handles and analyzes large volumes of data to create new ways to help clients sell products. Adknown is known for cutting edge technology and a friendly cooperative environment. Skilled data engineers, developers and analysts share knowledge freely. This supportive culture fosters creativity and growth and makes employees feel valued.

Job Description

In my continued role I expanded responsibilities to include data visualization with Grafana. I created and maintained dashboards for pipeline health while also maintaining and optimizing data pipelines using Python, SQL and AWS. The role required translating complex data into accessible insights in addition to strong programming and cloud skills. The dynamic scope encouraged constant learning and adapting which improved team effectiveness.

Learning Goals

Information literacy in data technologies

I enhanced literacy in Python, SQL and AWS by reading documentation, blogs and forums and applying best practices in projects. I asked my manager questions when needed. This improved accuracy and speed and increased confidence.

Data visualization skills

I improved skills in Grafana for monitoring. I built dashboards for key metrics and iterated based on feedback. The dashboard helped the team address issues quickly. I am now more confident using visualization tools.

Creative thinking

I worked on creative thinking for new features and coding challenges. I tried different approaches and drew ideas from open source projects. This helped me apply new ideas that improved performance.

Conclusions

This second term pushed me to advance data engineering and learn visualization skills. Combining technical accuracy with clear visuals made projects more effective and showed the impact of data driven decisions. The term strengthened adaptability and passion for technology and prepared me for future challenges.

Acknowledgments

I thank my manager Devin Rose for steady support and guidance. His advice was key to my growth. I appreciate his teaching within the role and his life advice. I am grateful to my coworkers at Adknown for a positive and supportive environment that helped me overcome new obstacles and reach goals.
