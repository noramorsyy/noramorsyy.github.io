Work Term Report Summer 2024

Data Engineer Intern at Adknown

Hi my name is Nora and I am in my third year at the University of Guelph majoring in Software Engineering and doing a double minor in Business and Project Management. This is my first co-op work term report. I will provide an overview of my experience working as a Data Engineer Intern at Adknown including projects, skills, challenges and growth.

About the Employer

Based in Guelph Ontario, Adknown is a digital media company that specializes in online advertising and data driven marketing solutions. The company processes and analyzes large volumes of data to help clients create innovative strategies. It is known for cutting edge technology and a positive friendly and cooperative work environment. Team members including data engineers, developers and analysts are skilled and open to sharing knowledge. This welcoming culture promotes creativity and continuous growth, makes employees feel valued and motivates strong performance.

Job Description

My role at Adknown was Data Engineer Intern working on data pipelines that process terabytes of data daily. I created and configured Airflow DAGs to automate data import from several sources into MySQL databases. Strong Python skills, SQL and familiarity with AWS were important. Many abilities were learned on the job and complemented fundamentals from my courses. Working in an agile environment with focus on efficiency, adaptability and continuous improvement was a key part of the role.

Learning Goals

Technical skills

I wanted to grow skills in Python, SQL, AWS services and related tools. I experimented with new techniques, set time to practice and applied insights to develop and optimize data pipelines which increased processing efficiency. Reaching this goal improved the quality of my work and prepared me to handle more complex tasks.

Communication

I prioritized clear communication. I kept my supervisor updated on progress and sought feedback often. I asked clarifying questions and incorporated suggestions. This supported alignment with project objectives and a collaborative friendly environment.

Problem solving

I aimed to improve problem solving in debugging and optimizing pipeline workflows. I analyzed issues, discussed options with my supervisor and used well thought out algorithms to improve performance. These steps helped me diagnose and fix problems quickly.

Time management

I worked on organization and time management by setting reasonable deadlines and prioritizing with Jira. Tracking progress helped me finish tasks ahead of schedule. This productivity let me handle multiple tasks effectively without sacrificing quality.

Conclusions

This work term was a transforming experience that let me apply theory in practice and build skills for my career. I learned the value of adaptability and continuous learning in data engineering. The contents of this report reflect my involvement and the value of the knowledge and experience I gained during this term.

Acknowledgments

I would like to acknowledge Devin Rose, my supervisor, for guidance and support during this term. His feedback and mentorship helped me refine my skills and approach to problem solving. I also acknowledge my colleagues at Adknown for a positive supportive environment that helped me improve and boosted my confidence and productivity.
